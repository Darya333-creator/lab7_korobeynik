OUR STORY

The idea was simple. Pets deserve food that is made with the same love, care, and thoughtfulness as we put into preparing meals for the human members of our families.

HELPING KEEP PETS HEALTHY AND HAPPY

We owe it to them to choose only the best ingredients like fresh meat and nutrient-rich superfoods. We keep processing to a minimum to preserve nutrients and flavour, cooking our recipes at the lowest possible temperature and using no meat meals.

COMMITTED TO PEOPLE AND THE PLANET

While pets are our number one priority, we also care deeply about being a force for good in our communities and reducing our footprint on the planet. We believe that committing to purposeful changes throughout our organization adds up to a big impact.
